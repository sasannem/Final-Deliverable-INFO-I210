    About Us

    We make digital windows for those who may not have actual windows in their space, or say someone already has windows but they don't like their view. That's where we come in. We can create just about any custom window view of your liking, or you can chose some of the sample options we have listed on our website. We have three sample categories from City, to Rural, and Animated. We either take our sample scence or custom made scene, render it out to the opitmal resolution for the clients display size. Along with the digital window your purchase will come with a free wooden frame, or you may use any other frame of your choosing.
